About Me

Hi, I'm Sean. I use words, photos, data and code to cover stories about the environment, oysters, earthquake weather ... and the occasional news story. My work has appeared in Bay Nature Magazine, KQED, the Ventura County Star and the Orange County Register.


In 2014, I graduated from the UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism (here's my alumni profile) and I won my fantasy soccer league.
